WebBrogue

Play Brogue the roguelike in your browser!

Chrome is strongly recommended. Other browsers (e.g. Firefox) should work but may be slower.

This site is based in the EU (London). Too much lag? - try the US site.

Brogue CE is a community-lead effort to keep improving Brogue, a game originally by Brian Walker. Downloadable builds are also available.

gBrogue is a fork of Brogue 1.7.4 by Greg Reed. unBrogue is a fork of Brogue 1.7.1 by Andrew Doull.

web-brogue is maintained by flend. Kip Raske built the original version and runs the US site. Fork the code on github, and post an issue or make a pull request.

Downloadable builds of v1.7.5 are available for Windows and Mac OS X v1.7.5 with important fixes made to the original v1.7.5 version.

Have fun and get that amulet and those lumenstones!
